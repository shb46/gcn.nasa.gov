# Microlensing Observations in Astrophysics (MOA)

**First Light:** 2000

**Final Alerts:** 2021

**Data Archives:**
[GCN Classic](https://gcn.gsfc.nasa.gov/moa_events.html)

[MOA](http://www2.phys.canterbury.ac.nz/moa/) is a project involving scientists in Japan and New Zealand to distribute microlensing events before their peak.
