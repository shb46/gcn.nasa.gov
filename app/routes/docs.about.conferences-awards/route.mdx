The GCN Team regularly presents updates and interacts with broader astronomy community at topical scientific conferences and meetings. Our scientists and developers are on hand to answer your questions and hear your feedback every year in the exhibit hall at the Winter meeting of the [American Astronomical Society](https://aas.org). Our booth includes an [educational, interactive demonstration](https://github.com/nasa-gcn/gcn-raspberry-pi-demo) of the fault-tolerant Apache Kafka data streaming technology behind GCN.
